🌅 Morning Study Timetable (5–7 Hours from 5:00 AM) : 

⏰ If You Study for 5 Hours (5:00 AM – 10:00 AM) : 

| Time                | Activity                            | Notes                              |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **5:00 – 5:15 AM**  | 🌬️ Freshen up & Light Warm-up      | Prepare your mind and body         |
| **5:15 – 6:15 AM**  | 📘 **Session 1 – Core Subject**     | Focus on difficult/new topics      |
| **6:15 – 6:25 AM**  | ☕ Short Break                       | Stretch, sip water or tea          |
| **6:25 – 7:25 AM**  | 📗 **Session 2 – Concept Building** | Continue with related topics       |
| **7:25 – 7:40 AM**  | 🍎 Light Breakfast                  | Healthy, not heavy                 |
| **7:40 – 8:30 AM**  | 📝 **Session 3 – Practice/Test**    | Solve questions or revise actively |
| **8:30 – 8:40 AM**  | 🚶 Break                            | Walk or relax                      |
| **8:40 – 9:30 AM**  | 📚 **Session 4 – Review/Recall**    | Summarize & revise                 |
| **9:30 – 10:00 AM** | 📒 Plan or Journal                  | Reflect, plan next session         |



⏰ If You Want 7 Hours (5:00 AM – 12:00 PM)
Add two extra focused sessions:

| Time                 | Activity                           |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **10:00 – 10:15 AM** | 🍵 Tea/Walk Break                  |
| **10:15 – 11:00 AM** | 📕 **Session 5 – Lighter Subject** |
| **11:00 – 12:00 PM** | 📖 **Session 6 – Mock/Test**       |





🌙 Night Study Timetable (4 Hours After 11 PM) : 


| Time                 | Activity                             | Notes                                 |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **11:00 – 11:15 PM** | 🧘‍♂️ Light relaxation & setup       | Calm your mind, set up your space     |
| **11:15 – 12:15 AM** | 📚 **Session 1 – Main Subject**      | Focus on the most difficult subject   |
| **12:15 – 12:25 AM** | ☕ Short Break                        | Stretch, hydrate, optional tea/coffee |
| **12:25 – 1:25 AM**  | 📘 **Session 2 – Secondary Subject** | A lighter subject or review work      |
| **1:25 – 1:35 AM**   | 🍎 Refresh Break                     | Snack or take a brief walk            |
| **1:35 – 2:20 AM**   | 📝 **Session 3 – Practice/Test**     | Solve problems, past papers, etc.     |
| **2:20 – 2:30 AM**   | 📒 Plan & Wind Down                  | Quick review + plan next session      |



✅ Tips for Night Study : 

1.Stay hydrated but avoid too much caffeine late.

2.Use blue light filters or night mode on screens.

3.Keep your room quiet and well-ventilated.

4.After 2:30 AM, try to sleep by 3:00 AM for better recovery.


✅ Morning Study Tips

5.Wake up and start within 15 minutes to not lose momentum.

6.Avoid distractions (keep phone away or on focus mode).

7.Eat a light, protein-rich breakfast around 7–8 AM.

8.Take screen-free breaks to rest your eyes.
